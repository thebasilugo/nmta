About Television Nollywood International

Our Mission

At Television Nollywood International (TNI), our mission is to celebrate, promote, and elevate the Nigerian film industry on a global scale. We are dedicated to showcasing the best of Nollywood talent, fostering creativity, and connecting filmmakers with audiences worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, TNI has grown from a small local initiative to a leading voice in the Nollywood industry. Our journey began with a passion for Nigerian cinema and a vision to create a platform that would bring recognition to the incredible talent within our borders.

Over the years, we have expanded our reach, launching the annual TNI Awards, our weekly digital magazine, and fostering partnerships with international film festivals and distributors. Today, we continue to evolve, always with the goal of pushing Nollywood to new heights.
