About NMTA

Celebrating the rich heritage and bright future of African cinema

Our Mission

The Nollywood Movie & Television Awards (NMTA) is dedicated to recognizing and celebrating excellence in Nollywood cinema and television. Our mission is to promote the growth of the Nollywood film industry, showcase emerging talent, and honor the outstanding achievements of filmmakers, actors, and industry professionals.

Our Vision

NMTA aims to be the premier platform for recognizing and celebrating excellence in African cinema, with a focus on Nollywood productions. We envision a future where Nigerian films and television productions are globally recognized and appreciated for their unique storytelling, cultural richness, and technical excellence.

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, NMTA has grown to become one of the most prestigious award ceremonies in the African entertainment industry. Over the years, we have honored hundreds of talented individuals and groundbreaking productions, helping to elevate Nollywood cinema on the global stage.
